# LiuYi_Oil-Painting_Method

It reads the whole book's method and reorganizes it into 20 sections across five parts (Philosophy → Materials → Technique → Color & Glazing → Art History), each written in both Chinese and English. Use the 中文 / 双语 / EN toggle in the sidebar to switch languages, and the search box to jump to any technique.
Five interactive tools I built to teach the trickiest ideas:

Fat-over-Lean simulator — drag each layer's oil content; break the rule and you watch the surface crack into alligator craquelure.
Three-Pile Flesh Lab (the signature one) — mix light/mid/shadow piles on a lit sphere, change the environment hue, and watch the nether gray shift toward its complement. Toggle "woodcut" to see the pre-blend stage and "corpse" to see why skin painted before its surroundings goes deathly pale.
Nether-Gray Wheel — a color wheel showing how the 冥灰色 sits opposite the environment, desaturated.
Glaze simulator — lay burnt sienna (or three others) over finished flesh; thin unifies, heavy chokes.
Pigment oil-absorption table — 19 pigments, sortable, showing which are fat/lean and fast/slow.
